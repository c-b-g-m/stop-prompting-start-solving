The Solve

Talk about your week.
See what's hiding in plain sight.

Most professionals have problems worth solving with AI but can't see them — they're hiding in the patterns of their work.

Speak for 90 seconds about your last week — meetings, tasks, things that took longer than you wanted. The Solve surfaces the recurring shapes, then plays a game with them.

No account needed. Recordings are sent to OpenAI for transcription and Anthropic for analysis, then discarded. Nothing is stored. This is the open-source version of a tool built for the Stop Prompting, Start Solving webinar.
